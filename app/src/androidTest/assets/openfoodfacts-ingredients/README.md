# Open Food Facts OCR fixtures

These 10 ingredient-label images are full-resolution variants of the records in
`docs/scanner_fixture_catalog.csv`. They are used only by Android instrumentation tests.

Source: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/

The Open Food Facts image responses identify the images as CC BY-SA 3.0. Product names,
barcodes, source pages, and selected-image URLs are preserved in the fixture catalog for
attribution and reproducibility.
