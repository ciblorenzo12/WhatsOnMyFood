What's On My Food Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

What's On My Food helps users scan food labels, analyze ingredients, maintain a pantry, and review retailer options. Ingredient and nutrition information is educational and is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Information we process

Account information may include your email address, display name, authentication provider identifier, and optional profile image. Product scans, ingredient text, pantry entries, preferences, and analysis results may be stored on your device. Camera or selected images are processed only when you use scanning or image-analysis features. Location is requested only when you choose nearby retailer features. Google Play supplies purchase status and transaction identifiers for subscription verification.

How information is used

We use this information to authenticate your account, provide scans and ingredient analysis, save your pantry, show nearby retailer searches, restore subscription access, prevent abuse, and maintain app reliability.

Service providers

The app uses Google Firebase for authentication and optional profile-image storage; Google Play for billing; Google ML Kit for on-device scanning, language identification, and translation; Google Gemini through our protected backend for optional AI analysis; Open Food Facts and other product data sources for product information; and retailer services when you request marketplace results. These providers process data under their own privacy terms.

Retention and deletion

Local pantry and scan data remains on your device until you remove it, reset app data, or delete your account. Account information remains in Firebase until your account is deleted. You can delete your account and associated profile image from Profile > Delete account. Successful deletion also clears local pantry, account, and contribution credentials from the device. Some transaction records may be retained by Google Play or as required by law. See the account deletion instructions.

Security and choices

Production network traffic uses HTTPS. You may deny camera, photo, or location access, although the related feature will not work. Do not submit confidential information in ingredient text or images.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact and updates

Questions or deletion problems can be sent to ciblorenzo@gmail.com. We may update this policy when app practices change; the effective date above will be revised.
