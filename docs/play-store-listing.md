# Google Play Store Listing Draft

## App name

What's On My Food

## Short description

Scan food labels, understand ingredients, and build a healthier pantry.

## Full description

What's On My Food helps you understand what is inside packaged food before it reaches your pantry.

Scan a barcode or ingredient label to review product details, ingredient concerns, nutrition signals, certifications, and practical alternatives. Save products to your pantry, search the additive database, and compare nearby retailer options.

Key features:

- Barcode and ingredient-label scanning
- Ingredient and additive explanations
- Rule-based health and nutrition signals
- Optional Bitwise AI summaries
- Pantry saving and risk insights
- Product certification and label information
- Retailer availability and alternative-product searches
- English, Spanish, and French interface support

Food, ingredient, and nutrition guidance is provided for general informational purposes only. It is not medical advice and must not be used to diagnose, treat, or prevent a medical condition. Consult a qualified healthcare professional about allergies, medical diets, or individual health concerns.

Some product information is supplied by third-party databases and may be incomplete or inaccurate. Always verify the physical package label when making allergy or health decisions.
