Delete your What's On My Food account

What's On My Food accounts are provided by Caleb Lorenzo.

Delete from the app

  1. Open What's On My Food and sign in.
  2. Open Profile.
  3. Select Delete Account and confirm.
  4. If Firebase requests a recent login, sign out, sign in again, and repeat the steps.

Request deletion without the app

Email the address below from the same email address used for your account. Use the subject “What's On My Food account deletion.” Do not send your password, purchase token, or identity documents.

Request account deletion

Data deleted and retained

Deletion removes the Firebase Authentication account and the profile image stored for that account. In-app deletion also clears pantry items, saved products, account preferences, and stored Open Food Facts contribution credentials from that device. Google Play may retain purchase and transaction records for billing, fraud prevention, or legal obligations. Requests are verified and processed within 30 days.
